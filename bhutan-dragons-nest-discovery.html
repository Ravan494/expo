Bhutan Dragons Nest Discovery

Commence a transformative journey through the mystical Kingdom of Bhutan.

This 6-day itinerary offers a curated experience, blending cultural immersion, breathtaking landscapes, and spiritual exploration.

Your adventure begins in the serene capital city of Thimphu, where you'll explore ancient monasteries, visit sacred sites, and witness the harmonious blend of tradition and modernity. From the majestic Tashicho Dzong, the seat of the Bhutanese government, to the tranquil Memorial Chorten, you'll immerse yourself in Bhutan's rich cultural heritage.

Continue your journey to the picturesque Punakha Valley, once the winter capital of Bhutan. Hike to the Chimi Lhakhang, a fertility temple renowned for its unique symbolism, and visit the magnificent Punakha Dzong, a stunning architectural masterpiece.

The highlight of your trip is the challenging yet rewarding hike to the iconic Tiger's Nest Monastery, perched high on a cliffside. Admire the breathtaking views of the Paro Valley and immerse yourself in the serene atmosphere of this sacred site.

As your Bhutanese adventure comes to an end, carry with you cherished memories of this extraordinary Himalayan kingdom.
