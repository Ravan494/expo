Meet Our Team

The People Behind the Pathways

Our dedicated team of program leaders, travel coordinators, and educators is passionate about creating transformational experiences. With decades of combined experience in global travel and education, we are here to support every step of your journey.

Sudarshan

Founder & Expedition Leader

Sudarshan completed his Post Grad in Hospitality and Tourism management. He worked in a production house as a location manager and an inbound luxury travel operator. With a degree from JNV university in Jodhpur, his passion for outdoor education inspires him to provide innovative programs in India and Nepal. He loves cricket and country music.

Sarah Jenkins

Director of Global Programs

Sarah brings over 15 years of experience in educational travel and risk management. She oversees the development of our immersive global programs with a careful eye for detail. Before joining Experiential Pathways, she spent a decade leading student groups across Southeast Asia and Bhutan. She believes travel is the ultimate classroom and is dedicated to fostering global citizenship among youth.

Rajeev Kumar

Senior Field Guide

An avid mountaineer and certified wilderness first responder, Rajeev has been exploring the Himalayas since he was a teenager. He leads our high-altitude programs. Rajeev is known for his incredible storytelling around the campfire and his deep knowledge of local flora and fauna. He ensures every student group stays safe while stepping entirely out of their comfort zone.
